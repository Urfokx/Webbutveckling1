Narbacular drop is a puzzle-platform game developed by nuclear monkey software which was made

by studens attending DigiPen institute of Technology. The gameplay consist of you taking control

of princess no-knees named that because she can't jump trapped by a demon but using portals from a

sentient creature named wally to solve puzzles and defeat the demon who trapped you there and escape.

The portals are only placeable on natural surfaces and prohibited on metal lava and other artificial

surfaces, you can also tell the portals apart with the color of their eyes.

Gabe newell the managing director of valve saw what the students had created and took an interest in their

teams work and hired them. The team and valve went to create the game portal using many of the same concepts
