You take control of either P-body or Atlas who are robots built for testing by GLaDOS and together

you and a friend solve tests with the help of eachother. You get your own portal device with different

colors to seperate and know which portals are yours and which are your friends. There are six different

courses to complete with your friend which are each about different elements but in some of the chambers

previous elements are used together to solve the test. There is also a seventh course which unlocks after

you complete the previous courses and has you completing chambers and eventually reach an old chassis with

an old prototype of GLaDOS being controlled mindlessy by a crow, but Atlas scare it away and P-body locks it.

out of the facility.
