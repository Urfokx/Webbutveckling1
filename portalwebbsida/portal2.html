Portal 2 is a first-person puzzle game developed by valve who also made portal 1 which is a prequel

to portal 2, like the previous game you use a portal gun to solve different puzzles with different

elements in them such as lasers, light bridges, gel, and a british person and more. You play as chell

navigating the ruined aperture science enrichment center from the events of the last game and also the

enrichment center when GLaDOS is fixed again completing tests.

Plot

You wake up from extended relaxation from a announcer who said it has been 9999999... years and Wheatley

a british personality core helps chell navigate the old test chambers from the last game and tries to

help you escape from the facility but accidentally reawakens GLaDOS a ruthless ai from the first game

chell defeated. Wheatley manages to break chell out of a chamber where they go behind the scences and

sabotage the turret production and the neurotoxin generator so GLaDOS can't use them anymore to try kill

Chell, Wheatley and Chell confront GLaDOS and do a core transfer where Wheatley now becomes the central

ai and GLaDOS is put in a potato, Chell and GLaDOS are then punched into the depths in an elevator. Chell

and GLaDOS now a potato work together to get to the surface after learning about the facility's history,

now back in the enrichment center they manage to defeat Wheatley and send him into space. GLaDOS now more

self aware lets Chell go and she finally escape aperture science.
