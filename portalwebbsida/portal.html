Portal is a puzzle-platform game released in 2007 by valve where you use a portal gun

to solve puzzles inside aperture science enrichment center whilst being watched by GLaDOS

Plot

The story follows chell as she is awaken from her stasis bed by a robotic ai voice coming from the

speakers about instructions of upcoming test and gives a welcome to the enrichent center. As Chell tests she

uses the aperture science handheld portal device to solve the tests with varying challanges and different

elements She is also promised cake after the conclusion of testing. After chell completes the final test

the ai manuevers her into an incinerator in a attempt to kill her but she uses the portal gun to escape

into the maintenace areas of the enrichment center whilst the ai pretends it wanted to kill chell. Chell

eventually finds a way through the maintenace tunnels and confornts the ai which name is GLaDOS

and blows up the facility after killing GLaDOS
